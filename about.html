About Us and our Team

Our Company

Mitchell Realty has been the top office in real estate sales production in the O'Fallon IL. MLS, since 2000, with agents that have decades of experience. Since inception, we have been an important part of the local community and we will continue to serve all of the area's real estate needs for years to come. With 14 agents in the office working under an experienced licensed broker, all of our staff members are high-caliber professionals with extensive experience and knowledge about our local real estate market. Our agents come from diverse backgrounds but all are dedicated to providing the highest quality service to our clients.

Our Owner

Jeuan Mitchell is the owner of Mitchell Realty and runs a team of dedicated professionals who are passionate about helping our clients find their dream homes. Our team is made up of experienced real estate agents, marketing specialists, and customer service experts who work together to provide the best possible experience for our clients.

Our Goals

Our primary goals are to provide exceptional service to our clients, build long-lasting relationships, and contribute positively to the community. We strive to be the go-to real estate agency in O'Fallon IL, known for our integrity, professionalism, and commitment to excellence.

Our Skills

Our team possesses a diverse set of skills that enable us to effectively serve our clients. From expert negotiation tactics to in-depth market analysis, we are equipped to handle all aspects of the real estate process. We continuously invest in training and development to stay ahead in the ever-changing real estate landscape.
